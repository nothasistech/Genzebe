About Genzebe-ገንዘቤ App

Genzebe is a simple and powerful income and expense tracking app designed for Ethiopian users. Manage your daily income and expenses with full support for Amharic and English languages, and switch between Ethiopian and Gregorian calendars anytime.

Track spending, stay organized, and take control of your finances.
